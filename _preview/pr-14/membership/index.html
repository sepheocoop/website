How to join as a member

Why?

You’re freelancer who wants to find collaborators or clients through our network, and help support the improvement of that network.

How?

  2. Register as an affiliate - if you’ve not done that already.

  3. Then on our Open Collective page, select the "Contribute" button on the "Member" option.

We’ll get in touch, invite you to join our members' Matrix chat, and give details of how to go from there.
