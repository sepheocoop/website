SEPHEO (Self-Employed People Helping Each Other) Coop is a mutual society for self-employed professionals. We make it easy for freelancers to connect with coops and other aligned organisations.

Are you a freelancer? Contracting? Self Employed?

If so, we are run by freelancers for freelancers. We aim to support people like you!

Freelancing can be rewarding, but it can also be challenging. It requires a breadth of skills beyond the core skillset for which you get paid. You need to be able to manage your business, your training, your networking and marketing.

Our insight is that co-operation is key to success. You have autonomy, but you also need support and a community.

If you feel like you would benefit from being supported by your peers - we think we can help you!

On the other hand, perhaps you’re ahead of the game? Experienced, well networked, and adept at keeping the plates in the air. Then please get in touch anyway! You can still benefit from connecting to people who may have complementary skills and resources.

Do you work with freelancers?

Organisations typically need to adapt to changing demands and turn-over in their membership.

We also aim to support you! A freelancer network is symbiotic with its customers.

As a co-operative, we especially love co-operative organisations. One of the principles of a co-operative is to co-operate with others. But we we work with all sorts of organisations, a diversity of aims and structures is to be celebrated.
