We are Sepheo!

Sepheo is mutual society for self-employed professionals.

  • For freelancers

  • For organisations

Sepheo what?

"Sepheo" stands for "Self-Employed People Helping Each Other"…​ we hope this explains itself!

We are a mutual support network run by freelancers for freelancers, providing a platform for skills exchange. We do this by:

  • Compiling a directory of freelancers, clients, and opportunities.

  • Actively creating networking opportunities.

  • Actively finding matches between clients and freelancers.

  • Facilitating building teams to tender for and deliver solutions.

  • Forging partnerships between service providers and freelancers.

We are self-funded, and organised democratically and accountably using the principles of the Rochdale Pioneers and the Solidarity Economy.

We currently achieve our core aims with minimal costs. In future, we have ambitious goals to expand our network, with the help of our members.

You can be a part of this by becoming a member

What we are not…​

If you are a freelancer: we do not supply your labour to clients - we support you, and introduce you to client organisations and other freelancers.

If you are an organisation: we do not deliver solutions or services to you - we introduce you to people who can.

After the initial introduction, for which we accept a fee to cover our costs, freelancers have a direct relationship with their clients.

Note that we can’t to train you to become a freelancer. But we do plan to partner with people who provide training in a range of areas.
