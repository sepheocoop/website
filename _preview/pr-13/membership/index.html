How to join as a member

Follow these steps:

  1. Start by creating an account on Open Collective, if you don’t have one. This is where we manage funds and pay expenses.

  2. Register as an affiliate - if you’ve not done that already (see above.)

  3. Then on our Open Collective page, logged in with your account there, select "Contribute" within the "Member" option.

You should receive a confirmation from Open Collective. If this is your first contact with us, we will be in touch.
